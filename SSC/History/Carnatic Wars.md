- Area around [[Cormandal Cost]]
- Between [[British]] and [[French]]
- from 1744 to 1763

# 1st Carnatic War
- Started in 1744
- Ended in 1748 with treaty of [[Aix-la-Chapalle]]
- General => [[Dupleix]]
- Won by [[French]]

# 2nd Carnatic War
- won by [[French]]
- General => [[Dupleix]]
- Started 1749
    - [[Battle of Ambur]]
    - Seize of [[Arcot]]
        - 1751
- Ended => 1754
    - [[Treaty of Pondicherry]]

# 3rd Carnatic War
- started 1756
- ended 1763
    - with [[Treaty of Paris]]
- British forces led by [[Eyre Coote]]
- French forces led by [[Comte de Lally]]
- Extension of 7 years war
- Known as [[Battle of Wandiwash (1760)]]
